# Acme Corp Marketing Playbook

## Strategy

Focus on developer-first content that demonstrates technical depth. Our target audience is engineering leaders (VP Engineering, CTO) at mid-market SaaS and fintech companies with 50 to 500 engineers. Goal for Q2 2026: 2x organic traffic and 50% increase in inbound demo requests vs Q1.

## Brand Voice

- Technical but accessible: write at the level of a senior engineer, not a marketer.
- Opinionated: take clear positions on developer experience, tooling, and workflows.
- Data-driven: support claims with benchmarks, case study metrics, and industry data.
- No buzzwords: avoid "synergy", "leverage", "paradigm shift". Use plain language.
